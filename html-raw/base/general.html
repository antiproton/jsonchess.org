Data structures

Colour

	
		"w" | "b"

Square

	
		0 .. 63

Squares are represented by numbers, with 0 at a1, 1 at b1, through to 63 at h8.

Timestamp

	
		Number

A point in time expressed as the number of milliseconds since the Unix epoch.

TimePeriod

	
		Number

A period of time in milliseconds.

Rating

	
		Number

A Glicko-2 rating.

PieceType

	
		"P" | "N" | "B" | "R" | "Q" | "K"

Result
